# Capability: teacher-feedback

## What it is

Teacher-side validation notes used to check whether FastPPT is solving real preparation pain points instead of abstract AI demo problems.

## Read when

- validating acceptance criteria
- choosing which polishing work matters
- preparing product or competition narrative

## Source of truth

- `docs/product/teacher-feedback.md`

## Current status

- `active`
- Good enough for direction
- Not yet broad enough to support statistical confidence

## Key conclusions

- High-frequency actions are updating stale content, adding recent cases, and editing specific pages.
- Teachers judge output by correctness, structure, personal teaching style fit, and traceability.
- Page-level update capability is more valuable than a flashy full-regeneration story.

## Upstream / downstream

- Upstream: interviews and live feedback capture
- Downstream: revise loop, evidence attachment, Mode A prioritization
